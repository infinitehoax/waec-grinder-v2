# 📖 User Guide: Mastering the WAEC Grinder

Welcome to the **WAEC Grinder**, the ultimate tool for crushing your exams through focused, high-repetition study.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Enter Your Name**: Used for your local study profile and multiplayer rooms.
2. **Select Subjects**: Choose one or more subjects you want to study.
3. **Choose Study Mode**:
   - **OBJ Only**: Multiple-choice questions.
   - **Theory Only**: Essay-style questions with AI grading.
   - **Both (Recommended)**: A mix of both for balanced preparation.
4. **Set Batch Size**: How many questions per round. Default is 5.
5. **Exam Mode (CBT)**: Toggle this for a real exam experience (timed, no instant feedback).

---

## 🔄 The Grinder Engine (Spaced Repetition)

The app uses two main queues to ensure you master every question:
- **Unseen Queue**: Questions you haven't seen yet.
- **Failed Queue**: Questions you got wrong.

**The Golden Rule**: Failed questions reappear in the very next batch. You cannot finish a subject until your failed queue is empty and all unseen questions have been mastered.

---

## ✍️ Answering Questions

### OBJ Questions
- Select an option (A, B, C, or D).
- In standard mode, you get instant feedback. Green for correct, Red for wrong with an explanation.
- In **Exam Mode**, your answer is saved, and you'll see the results in the final Report Card.

### Theory Questions
- Type your answer in the text area for each sub-question.
- Your drafts are **auto-saved** as you type.
- Click **"Submit for AI Grading"** to get granular feedback.
- **💡 Explain Simpler**: Stuck on an explanation? Click this button to have the AI explain it in simpler terms.

---

## 🏆 Gamification & Tracking

### Achievements
Unlock achievements by hitting milestones:
- **Efiko**: Study between midnight and 4:00 AM.
- **Speed Demon**: Finish 10 OBJ questions in under 2 minutes.
- **Einstein's Heir**: Master 50 Physics questions.
- Check the **Achievements** tab on the dashboard to see your collection!

### Streaks
Study every day to maintain your streak. Reach 7, 14, or 30 days for special badges.

### Topic Mastery
The dashboard shows your performance by topic. Focus on your "Weakest Areas" to improve your overall score.

---

## 🤝 Multiplayer Mode

Study with friends in real-time!
- **Create a Room**: Select subjects and mode, then share the Room ID.
- **Host Controls**: The host can set the question count, time limit, and anti-cheat settings.
- **Anti-Cheat**: If enabled, leaving the tab or window will flag you to the host.
- **Filter Mastered**: Choose to only play questions no one in the room has mastered yet.

---

## ⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Enter**: Next Question / Next Batch / Restart Session.
- **D**: Return to Dashboard (on the Report Card).
- **1, 2, 3, 4**: Select options A, B, C, D (OBJ mode).
